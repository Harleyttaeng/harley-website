ABOUT

My name is Harley Ding, a few years after spending nights and weekends creating content and producing websites for struggling friends, colleagues, and their referrals while building startup companies full-time in San Francisco. Inspired by clients' happiness when empowered to easily share their work with the world, she decided to leave her job to launch a business dedicated to helping people and companies shine online.
